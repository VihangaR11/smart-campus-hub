FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is my data private?

Yes. SmartStudent Hub has no backend or server — everything you enter (tasks, moods, budget, forum posts) is stored only in your own browser via localStorage. Nothing leaves your device.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There's no login or sign-up anywhere in the app. Just open a page and start using the tools.

Will my data sync across devices?

No — because there's no server, your data lives on the device and browser you used. Opening the app on another device starts fresh.

How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Use the sun/moon button in the top-right of the navigation bar. Your choice is remembered on your next visit.

How do I reset a tool's data?

Clear your browser's site data for this site, and the tools return to their starting state. Handy before a fresh demo.

Which browsers are supported?

Any modern browser — Chrome, Edge, Firefox or Safari. The layout is fully responsive on desktop and mobile.
