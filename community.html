Community FAQ

Good to know

Do I need an account to post?

No. This is a demo forum with no login or backend — just type a name (or stay anonymous) and post. Everything is saved locally in your own browser.

Where are posts stored?

In your browser's localStorage. They persist on this device between visits, but they aren't shared to other people or devices, since there's no server.

How do I clear the forum?

Clearing your browser's site data for this page resets it to the seed posts. Handy right before a fresh demo.

What are the community guidelines?

Keep it kind, keep it useful, and keep it relevant to student life. Choose a category so others can find your post.
